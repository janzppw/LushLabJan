5 Ways to Decorate with LushLab Products

From bedside lamps to cute plant pots, we’ve rounded up some dreamy ways to use your favorite pastel pieces around your room.

1. **Bedside Glow-Up**: Place the Saturn Glass Ball Lamp on your nightstand for a soft, celestial vibe.
2. **Shelfie Goals**: Stack your favorite books between the decorative bookends and drop a mini plant pot beside them for peak aesthetic.
3. **Desk Decor**: Add a doll or lamp next to your pen holder to soften the workspace.
4. **Window Sill Charm**: Line up 2-3 pastel pots and let nature light make them glow.
5. **Mood Corners**: Use fairy lights and a mix of LushLab items to create your own cozy, Instagram-worthy nook.

Why Pastels are the New Power Palette

Pastel colors aren’t just cute — they’re calming, expressive, and totally in style. From baby pink to soft lavender and minty green, pastels make a space feel lighter, happier, and more personal.

They also work well with minimalist designs, making them perfect for anyone who loves clean, gentle aesthetics. Whether it’s in fashion, decor, or web design (hello, LushLab), pastels are here to stay.
